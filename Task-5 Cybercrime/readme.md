Stay Safe Online – Cyberbullying & General Safety Tips (for Indian college students)

1.	Do not respond to abusive messages or comments; take screenshots as evidence instead.

2.	Use strong privacy settings on social media so only trusted people can see your posts.

3.	Block and report accounts that send threats, hate, or repeated harassment.

4.	Never share your passwords or OTPs with friends or classmates; change them regularly.

5.	Think before you post – avoid sharing content that can be misused or embarrass you later.

6.	Talk to a trusted friend, mentor, or counselor if you are being cyberbullied; do not suffer in silence.

7.	For suspicious payment links, UPI requests, or “prize” messages, verify directly with the official app or bank before acting.

8.	Never approve UPI collect requests from unknown numbers, and always double-check the receiver name before paying.

9.	Report serious cyberbullying, fraud, or threats on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.
