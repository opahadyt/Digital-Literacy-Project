Do’s
•	Do use your real name and a clear profile where needed for academic or professional purposes.
•	Do think before you post and consider how content may look to professors or recruiters.
•	Do use strong privacy settings and regularly review who can see your posts.
•	Do verify news and information from reliable sources before sharing.
•	Do be respectful and polite in comments, messages, and group discussions.


Don’ts
•	Don’t share personal information like address, phone number, or IDs publicly.
•	Don’t post abusive, discriminatory, or offensive content about anyone.
•	Don’t share screenshots of private chats or emails without consent.
•	Don’t click suspicious links or share OTPs and passwords on social platforms.
•	Don’t mix academic/professional accounts with inappropriate memes or content.
